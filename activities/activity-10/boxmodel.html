The Best food in the Philippines

Sinigang is a popular Filipino sour and savory soup, characterized by its tangy broth, often made with tamarind, and filled with meat (like pork, beef, or seafood) or vegetables, served with steamed rice. It's a versatile, one-pot meal, with the sourness coming from various agents like tamarind, guava, or unripe mangoes, and it includes ingredients like tomatoes, onions, and leafy greens. egetables, and a tangy broth.

Questions and Answers

What is normal flow in HTML?
Normal flow is how the browser lays out elements by default: block elements (like divs) stack vertically, and inline elements (like spans) sit side-by-side. Elements follow the order they appear in the HTML code.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?
Float pushes an element to the left or right, allowing text to wrap around it. You must "clear" a float because floated elements are taken out of the normal document flow, which can cause the parent container to collapse or following elements to overlap.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?
Relative shifts an element without affecting its neighbors. Absolute positions an element relative to its nearest "positioned" ancestor. Fixed sticks an element to the viewport, so it stays in place even when you scroll.
